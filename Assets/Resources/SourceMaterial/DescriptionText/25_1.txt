Vanni hurls an obscenity at God and the serpents swarm over him. The centaur Cacus arrives to punish the wretch; he has a fire-breathing dragon on his shoulders and snakes covering his equine back. 

Dante then meets five noble thieves of Florence and observes their various transformations. One in human form is merged with a six-legged serpent. Another figure first appears as a man, but exchanges forms and bites at another in the form of a four-footed serpent.
