You see before you those whose attitude toward material goods deviated from virtue. They are punished in this the fourth circle of Hell. The damned here include the avaricious or miserly - including many clergymen, and popes and cardinals - who hoarded possessions, and the prodigal, who squandered them. The hoarders and spendthrifts are forced to joust, using as weapons great weights that they push with their chests.
